Your Raleigh Movers

TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® has served the Raleigh-Durham community that we all proudly call home since 1998. Our mission has always been to provide the very best experience to our customers while making the workplace a great place to be for our entire moving family. Our entire company takes great pride in our trucks, our reputation, and making sure that each and every customer gets our best each and every time. TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® is the only choice for local or long-distance relocation for all of our Raleigh, Wake Forest, Morrisville, Knightdale, and Cary customers. We look forward to showing you what a professional moving experience looks like!

Stress-free moving services

Our team offers a large array of services including residential and business moving, as well as local and long-distance services. Feel free to contact one of our specialists to schedule a no obligation, in-home consultation. If you require short-term or long-term storage, we can handle that, too! Our team of move professionals are second to none as all of them complete a stringent background screening before they are hired, and complete more than 40 hours of classroom and hands-on mover training before they ever step onto one of our 25 trucks.

Moving Services

Local moving

Local moving is what started our brand more than 30 years ago, and we take pride in exceeding our customers’ expectations! We offer a professionally trained and background checked moving team to ensure your belongings are handled with care and that your move runs smoothly.

Long-distance moving

Whether you’re moving across town or across the country, we’ll get you there. We specialize in long-distance moving, keeping your items safe and secure along the way with the same customer service excellence our reputation was built upon.

Packing Services

Having more than 30 years of experience as the leaders in the moving industry, we’ve learned a thing or two when it comes to packing efficiently. We offer a variety of packing services, from just a few items to an entire home or business. If you’re looking to do it yourself, our locations carry all of the necessary supplies.

Storage

If you’re in between homes, completing home renovations, or just need a solution for items that won’t fit inside your home, finding a place for your items can be tricky. Our onsite storage units can provide you with storage flexibility, short or long-term.

Student Storage

As a college student, storage space is a premium, and finding effective ways to store your belongings can alleviate some of the stress that comes with moving in and out of student housing. We have the space to store your belongings in between semesters, or even during the school year.

Boxes & Supplies

Ensuring you have the right supplies and boxes to complete your next home or business move is crucial to the success of the move, and luckily we’ve got you covered. We offer a wide selection of competitively priced moving boxes and packing supplies at our locations all across the country.

THE TRIANGLE'S BEST COLLEGE MOVERS

College Moving is one of the many specialties of our Raleigh area TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® locations. Our Triangle facilities have been providing outstanding packing and moving services since 1998 and in that time, we have learned a thing or two about providing great moving services to all of the Raleigh-Durham area universities. That is why we are thrilled to offer expert moving services to NC State, Duke, and Carolina students that works with their busy schedules. Our moving teams will pick up all of your college student's belongings and transport it to our local climate-controlled storage facility for the summer.  When school starts again in the fall, we will deliver to their new dormitory or apartment at a time that is convenient for you! Call us today and ask one of our sales team members about how to set up your College Move!

We are the moving experts

TWO MEN AND A TRUCK is extremely active throughout the greater Raleigh-Durham market as we continue to enjoy success as the premier mover in the Triangle.  Customers like you voted us Best of the Triangle again in 2018 and we feel that our success is based on our community-focused commitment to provide the very best moving services in the Raleigh area.  Our team is heavily involved in multiple area Chambers of Commerce, the Triangle Apartment Association, as well as with Raleigh, Durham, and Chapel Hill Realty groups.  Our team has been an Angie's List Super Service Award recipient on numerous occasions and our team posts an impressive 4.8 stars in Google searches.  Our record certainly speaks for itself!

Catering to TRiangle seniors

Let's face it – we are all aging, and with that comes relocating to new homes with fewer stairs and less of a lawn to maintain. TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® specializes in moving of all types, and our entire family has an affinity for assisting seniors with their specific needs.  With that focus in mind, our entire team has completed training with the Orange County Department on Aging to make sure that we never miss a beat when moving seniors. 

That means that if you need assistance with packing, in-home rearranging, or moving to a retirement community, give TWO MEN AND A TRUCK of Raleigh and Durham a call!

Making the most of every relocation

TWO MEN AND A TRUCK® is the only call to make for your relocation needs! If you need a small move across town or a move to the west coast, we have the experienced team members who can handle it. All of our moving professionals are trained to help our customers throughout the entire move process. Packing, moving, and unpacking are training essentials for TWO MEN AND A TRUCK®, not services that are out of the ordinary. Unlike many moving companies, our rates – whether local or long-distance – are all-inclusive, which means there are no hidden charges for stairs or long walks. Call, email, or text us today and we will help you make the most out of your next RDU area relocation!
